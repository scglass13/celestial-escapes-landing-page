Lunar Luxury

Embark on an extraordinary journey to the Moon with Lunar Luxury, our signature space vacation package designed for adventurers and dreamers alike. Your experience begins with a breathtaking launch from Earth aboard our state-of-the-art spacecraft, equipped with luxurious accommodations and panoramic viewing decks. Once on the Moon, you’ll explore the serene landscapes of the lunar surface, from the iconic Sea of Tranquility to the stunning peaks of Tycho Crater. Enjoy guided excursions, lunar rovers for exploration, and even the chance to leave your own footprint on the Moon. Your trip includes:

  • Round-trip transport in our cutting-edge space shuttle.
  • A 3-day stay in our exclusive Moon Base with zero-gravity lounges and private suites.
  • Gourmet meals inspired by Earth’s finest cuisines.
  • Guided moonwalks and optional adventure tours.
Return to Earth with memories that are truly out of this world.
